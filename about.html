About

Building a more transparent provincial governments through an open budget portal.

An Open Data Initiative of Public Procurement Monitoring Office to create a single repository of data on public procurement to the public.

The provincial budget portal contains the province level budget allocation and spending data published by the provincial government. The portal aims to create an effective use of public information to demand accountability, geared for institutional improvement, efficient services delivery, and an equitable society.

Our Missions

  • check_circle To inform people, the policy makers how the budgets have been allocated and spent in the provinces
  • check_circle Create buzz on the province budget
  • check_circle Help the development partners, students, researchers, policy enthusiasts, journalists to know more on the provincial budget
  • check_circle Help people raise questions to the policy makers, know the priority of provinces.
